Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

          

            
How do I filter data on the monthly statistics page?

            
Use the dropdown menus at the top of the 'Monthly Statistics' page to select a specific year, month,
              device, or camera. The charts and stats will update automatically.

          

          

            
Can I export a report?

            
Yes. On both the 'Monthly Statistics' and 'Daily Statistics' pages, there is a "Download Report" button.
              This will generate a comprehensive PDF report based on your current filters.
